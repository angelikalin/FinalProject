About Us

我們創立於民國91年3月16日，為全南投唯一的神經外科專科診所。這個名字的靈感源於草屯鎮的舊名「草鞋墩」，而舊名於《草屯鎮志》中所記載：以前為各種人出入門戶的地點，乃為草鞋製造及集散地，夙為換氣新舊草鞋之地，日積月累，草鞋成墩因而得名。最初我們在育英街的小小租處街道上開業，轉眼間搬了一次到太平路二段66號，直到後來2011年，搬遷至目前的太平路二段28號，有了屬於我們自己的診所。

First opened in March 16th, 2003, this clinic has been the only clinic specialized in Nerosurgic presented in Nantou, Taiwan. The name of 'Csao Sei Dun', which translated to be grass-sandals pier, came from the past where people had a high demand in changing their shoes at this town before heading out to larger cities or coming back from other places.
We moved twice in this town, from smaller location to finally having our own place, Taiping Rd Section 2, 28th, here in 2011.
